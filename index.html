What this is

The Next Mountain Readiness Assessment surfaces the gap between where you are and where you need to be as a senior leader in the AI era — without telling you what to do. It does three things:

  • Names where you stand on AI. Not where the headlines say you should — where you actually do.
  • Tests your readiness across eight dimensions. Essence, vision, discernment, human advantage, leverage, team trust, and weekly rhythm.
  • Gives you a private report. Your zone, your strongest dimension, your growth dimension, and three questions worth sitting with.

Who built this

The assessment is a tool of Next Mountain Circles, a 90-day cohort program for senior leaders learning to lead wisely in the age of AI. Six one-hour Circles on Zoom, one full day in person at Whisper Ranch in Boulder, Colorado.
